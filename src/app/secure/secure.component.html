Secure Shipment

Secure shipment is a service that protects shippers against lost, stolen, or damaged packages.

If an insured package does not reach its destination, or if it is damaged when it's delivered,
then the shipper is reimbursed the declared value of the items in the package.
Secure shipment service charges will be 0.49% (plus GST) of the invoice value.
