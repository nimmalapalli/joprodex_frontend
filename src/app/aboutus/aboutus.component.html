About Us

NimbusPost is the fastest-growing community of start-ups and entrepreneurs coming together for quick and hassle-free shipping solutions with the widest reach and reliable customer support.

The present shipping solutions lack in providing transparent information for businesses to make an informed decision. NimbusPost bridges that gap to facilitate a decision backed up with reasons.

We bring together all the courier partners on one platform to help you pick the best suiting your requirements after comparing the offerings from each.

Headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, we are currently serving across more than 29000 pin codes, with 1 Lac+ happy clients.
